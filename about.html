On the Framework

Why Human Value Governance™ exists

We have data governance. We have model governance.
We do not have a governance discipline for protecting human qualities from being quietly optimized away by AI systems. That gap has a name now.

Human Value Governance™ was built from direct experience inside organizations that deployed AI into consequential workflows — hiring, performance management, customer service — without asking what human value those systems were allowed to erase.

The framework is not theoretical. It names a governance failure already visible in production environments, defines the six human qualities that must be protected, and establishes seven covenants that translate principle into auditable institutional commitment.
